Privacy Policy

How this portfolio handles information.

This portfolio is designed to collect only the minimum information needed to respond to contact requests. The page is kept simple so visitors can quickly understand what is collected, how it is used, and how to reach out with questions.

Summary

What is collected

  • Name and email address if you submit the contact form.
  • Message content you choose to send.
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and page requests through normal hosting logs.
Use

How the information is used

  • To reply to your message.
  • To understand the type of project or request you have.
  • To improve the website experience and maintain the site.
Storage

How information is stored

Messages sent through the contact form may be delivered to an email inbox or another communication channel used to manage responses. They are retained only as long as needed for communication and follow-up.

Sharing

Information sharing

Personal details submitted through the site are not intended for public display. They are not sold, and they are only shared if needed to respond to the request or comply with a legal obligation.

Cookies

Cookies and local storage

This site may use local storage for preferences such as theme selection. Standard hosting and analytics tools may also use cookies or similar technologies depending on your browser and the services configured on the site.

External services

Third-party services

If the site includes external links, embedded content, or third-party forms in the future, those services may collect information under their own policies. Review those policies separately when using them.

Contact

Questions about privacy

If you have a question about this policy or how your information is handled, please use the contact page and send a direct message.
